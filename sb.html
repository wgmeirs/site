Essex Dog Park Grand Opening!

It's Finally Here! Essex Dog Park will be celebrating its Grand Opening this Saturday, June 29th from 10am to noon.

We will have a leash cutting ceremony, refreshments and a treat, for both you and your pup! We hope you and your dog(s) can be there to celebrate with us as our park transistions from the planning stages to dogs running and playing.

Chittendon County Rabies Clinic

The Humane Society of Chittenden County will be offering a Rabies Clinic Saturday, September 11 from 1:00pm to 4:00pm right here at HSCC in South Burlington. Vaccinations and certificates will be provided by Dr. Susan McMillan of Vet to Pet Mobile Veterinary Service and the Old North End Veterinary Clinic for $10 (a 3-year vaccine requires proof of prior vaccination via rabies certificate -otherwise a one-year vaccine will be given.) Cats must be in carriers and dogs on leash. Please keep in mind that due to the discounted price there may be a large crowd and you may be asked to wait. Please be patient and enjoy some free ice cream provided by Ben & Jerry's while you wait!
